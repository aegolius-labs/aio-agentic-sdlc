# Agentic Backlog Rules

## Handling Feature Ideas

If you conceptualize new features, improvements, or non-critical refactoring ideas during your workflow, you MUST NOT assume immediate implementation or drift from your current scope of work. Instead, you MUST log the idea to the backlog using the `aio-agentic-sdlc` tool (MCP or CLI fallback) with an appropriate effort and impact score so it can be formally prioritized.

You MUST ensure the backlog is sorted after adding dependencies.
